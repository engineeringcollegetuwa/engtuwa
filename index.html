About us

Engineering College Tuwa, was established in year 2009 by Panchmahal Jilla Shikshan Pracharak Mandal, Morva (Rena). Institute in the first self-financed engineering college established at the one of the backward district of the state. At present institutes offer graduate and diploma programs in almost all major branches like Mechanical, Civil, Computer, Electrical and Electronics & Communication Engineering.
